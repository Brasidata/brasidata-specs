# Brasidata Open Specs

This repository centralizes **engineering, naming, and infrastructure standards** adopted by Brasidata across its projects and services.

These specifications aim to ensure consistency, maintainability, and interoperability across all Brasidata’s open and internal software ecosystems.

## Language Policy

All **public repositories, documentation, and specifications** of Brasidata **must be written in English**.  
Internal or private repositories may use Portuguese when targeting internal teams or local clients.
